When will my order be delivered?

Due to COVID-19 restrictions, the time to pickup all the items is longer than usual. Your order will be delivered within 4 hours. As the lockdowns are relaxed, we will expect improvements in timeline. Any order placed, after 7:00 pm will be delivered the next day morning.

Your approximate delivery date can be found in your order confirmation email. You will also receive an email on the day of your delivery letting you know when your order is on its way.

How can I cancel an order?

Yash Farm to Table is committed to providing exceptional products and service to its customers. Unfortunately, when one appointment cancels without giving enough notice, they prevent another customer from getting their own delivery/pickup package. Customers have the option to cancel their order within 2 hours of placing your order. A full refund will be issued back via their original method of payment. Cancellation requests that are made with after 2 hours of placing the order will not be entertained. For additional details involving your transaction, please visit our Online Sales Agreement.
